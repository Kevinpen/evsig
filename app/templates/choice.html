Evolutionary signatures describe the extent to which molecular features of intrinsically disordered regions deviate from our null expectation in their evolution. If a molecular feature deviates in its evolution from our null expectation, we predict it is under selection, and thus may be important for the function of the IDR in question. Evolutionary signatures can be used as a tool to generate hypotheses about molecular features that could be important for the function of individual IDRs.
